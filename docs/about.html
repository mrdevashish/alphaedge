About Infinity Invest

Founded by Brajesh Maniyar (Pune), Infinity Invest is a small, practical outfit focused on process-driven investing.

  • Psychology first: we design tactics that are easy to follow under pressure.
  • Evidence over opinions: ideas must earn their place via data and journaling.
  • Compounding: fewer, better decisions; steady habits beat hot tips.

We are not SEBI registered advisors. Everything here is for education only.

Working style

  • Playbooks for entries/exits and risk budgets.
  • Weekly prep → daily checklist → short debrief.
  • “Capital first” rules to cut regret & revenge trades.
