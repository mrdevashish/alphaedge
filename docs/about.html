Brajesh Maniyar — Trading research, made understandable.

I’m Brajesh Maniyar, a Pune-based trading researcher focused on turning noisy market data into clear, rule-based suggestions. My work combines disciplined risk management with plain-language explanations so that decisions feel calm, not rushed. I don’t promise certainty—I build repeatable processes and let probability do the heavy lifting.

Credibility & approach

  • Works with systematic checklists: trend strength, momentum, volume context, and risk-to-reward.
  • Writes “why it works / when it fails” notes for every setup to set correct expectations.
  • Believes small, consistent edges + tight risk beats one-off “hero” trades.
  • Keeps UIs clean so you focus on levels, not noise.

How suggestions are built

  1. Scan large-cap & liquid mid-cap universes for trend + momentum alignment.
  2. Filter by volatility bands and structure (breaks/retests).
  3. Define entry, stop, and targets before the trade; never after.
  4. Annotate confidence with simple language (what strengthens / weakens the idea).
  5. Review outcomes to refine the checklist—not to chase hindsight wins.

What you’ll get here

  • Crisp, readable levels (Entry / SL / Targets) with confidence notes.
  • A consistent format across ideas—easy to compare and journal.
  • Transparent language about uncertainty and risk.
