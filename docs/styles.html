Trading Styles (Basics)

Intraday

Same-day entries and exits. Needs quick decisions and strict SL.

  • Pros: frequent opportunities
  • Cons: higher noise

Swing

Hold 2–10 days on structure breaks/retests.

  • Pros: calmer pace
  • Cons: gap risk

Positional

Multi-week trends; focus on levels & risk.

  • Pros: fewer decisions
  • Cons: patience needed

Breakout

Enter on strength; SL below structure.

Mean Reversion

Fade extremes back to average; needs tight exits.

Trend Follow

Ride higher highs/lows; add on pullbacks.
